Custom Pricing

Manage's solution program offers pricing based on the individual needs in your organization. Our custom pricing includes all IT related services. This style of service is what differentiates Manage from our competitors. Our comprehensive plan includes both remote and on-site support, project work, and consulting services. We also offer additional services such as remote monitoring, backup and antivirus protection. We work hard to provide the best solutions based on your needs.

Reliable Flat Fees

Manage's Service fees are based on the number of employees using computers in your organization, not the number of hours we spend fixing problems. This puts the burden on us to make sure you experience as few problems as possible. The result is a highly efficient network and predictable affordable IT Services. Manage is a vendor neutral IT provider that is not motivated to push our clients toward any particular solution or any particular product. We simply recommend the most cost effective, proven solutions.

Reduced Labor Costs

Finding the right mix of internal IT staff and outside IT help can be difficult and very expensive. No one wants to pay for their employees down time, but not having enough staff can be just as costly. Consultants and outside help can charge extremely high hourly rates. Add in the cost of managing and recruiting IT help and the costs can get out of hand quickly. With Manage’s Managed Solutions team, you have immediate access to the knowledge and experience of a whole department of IT professionals often for less than the cost of a single employee.
